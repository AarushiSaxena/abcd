Globally, the number of people with visual impairment is very high. They
face difficulties doing their day-to-day activities, especially during social
interactions. In order to assist visually impaired people, we introduce a new
system to identify the emotion of the confronting person, recognize currency
and detect objects in close proximity-all in one!
In this system the blind person uses camera assistance for image acquisition
of the subject and by using an audio device, the situation is conveyed,
making it convenient to the user. Creating a Vision without Sight!
